📚 About Sengar's Gallery

Our Mission

Sengar's Gallery is dedicated to preserving and promoting traditional Indian handicrafts and art forms.

We work directly with master artisans and craftspeople from Rajasthan and across India to bring you authentic, high-quality pieces that tell stories of culture, heritage, and artistic excellence.

Our Collections

We specialize in 5 major traditional art forms:

  • Bani Thani - Rajasthani courtly paintings
  • Pichwai - Krishna temple paintings
  • Madhubani - Bihar's sacred folk art
  • Warli - Tribal geometric patterns
  • Miniature Paintings - Mughal era artwork

Why Choose Us?

  • ✨ Authentic Art - Directly from master artisans
  • 🎨 Quality Guaranteed - Meticulous craftsmanship
  • 🏛️ Cultural Heritage - Preserving traditions
