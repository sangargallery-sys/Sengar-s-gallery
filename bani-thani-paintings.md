# Bani Thani Paintings - Traditional Rajasthani Art

## About Bani Thani Art Style

Bani Thani is a distinctive form of traditional Rajasthani folk art that originated in Jodhpur, Rajasthan. Named after a legendary courtesan and dancer of the 18th century, this art form is characterized by its vibrant colors, intricate details, and depictions of courtly life and traditional themes.

### Key Characteristics
- **Vibrant Colors**: Rich use of reds, golds, greens, and blues
- **Fine Brushwork**: Extremely detailed and delicate linework
- **Traditional Patterns**: Geometric designs and floral motifs
- **Cultural Themes**: Depicting women, festivals, court scenes, and daily life
- **Emotional Expression**: Beautiful portrayal of human emotions and relationships
